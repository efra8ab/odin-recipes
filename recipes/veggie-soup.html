Veggie Soup

Finished Veggie Soup

Warm and healthy for those rainy days at home

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1 zucchini, chopped
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook for about 3 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the garlic, carrots, and celery and cook for another 3–4 minutes.
  4. Stir in the diced potato, zucchini, and green beans.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and vegetable broth.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  7. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
  8. Cook until all the vegetables are tender.
  9. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  10. Serve hot and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
