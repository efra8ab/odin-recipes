Enchiladas

Finished Enchiladas

A classic mexican dish, delicious and spicy!

Ingredients:

    Tortillas & Filling
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cooked shredded chicken or ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil for frying
    Red Enchilada Sauce
  • Dried guajillo chiles
  • Dried ancho chiles (optional)
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cumin
  • Mexican oregano
  • Salt
  • Chicken broth
    Toppings
  • Queso fresco or cotija cheese
  • Mexican crema
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Avocado

Steps:

  1. Remove stems and seeds from the dried chiles and place them in hot water for about 10 minutes to soften.
  2. Blend the softened chiles with tomato, garlic, onion, cumin, oregano, salt, and chicken broth until smooth to make the red enchilada sauce.
  3. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan and lightly fry the corn tortillas for a few seconds on each side to soften them.
  4. Fill each tortilla with shredded chicken or ground beef and roll them tightly.
  5. Place the rolled tortillas in a baking dish.
  6. Pour the red enchilada sauce evenly over the enchiladas.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 15–20 minutes until heated through.
  8. Remove from the oven and top with queso fresco or cotija cheese, Mexican crema, cilantro, and avocado before serving.
